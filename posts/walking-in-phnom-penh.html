Walking in Phnom Penh

April 15, 2016

Phnom Penh has a surreal quality that transforms you out of the corporate reality. Not like an all-inclusive resort on a tropical beach — more like an alternative reality.

After grabbing a tuk tuk to downtown from the airport (about 45 minutes), I realized that I was indeed in the modern day "wild west".

I bought a sim card for $1.50 a week (a half price deal).
