Koh Rong – A Tropical Island Paradise

April 15, 2016

Located about 40 minutes from Sihanoukville off the southern coast of Cambodia in the Bay of Thailand, Koh Rong is truly an undiscovered tropical island paradise — white sandy beaches, warm crystal-clear ocean waters, and a hot tropical climate.

Our hotel was a 5-minute walk up a hill from the beach where we arrived, with a nice view back over the water toward Sihanoukville.

We encountered a few bars and restaurants staffed by young backpacker nomads in exchange for free food and rent. There seemed to be fewer than 100 tourists on the island — more like about 40.

We joined Adam's Adventure for $25 — an all-day boat trip around the island. We visited a fisherman's village, then had lunch on an uninhabited beach with our group of about 20 people representing various countries including France, Argentina, Israel, Mexico, and Belgium.
